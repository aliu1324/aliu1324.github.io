Molecular structure VSEPR 3D simulator

Created by Andrew Liu, student at Columbia University CHEM UN1403 General Chemistry I


Notes

  • When entering a molecule, enter the central atom first, and include all subscripts (including 1). Also, enter a space before entering the net molecular charge (e.g. N1H4 +1).
  • The current version does not detect errors in your input, so if there is an error in your input and the program stops, just refresh the page and try again.
  • When the 3D simulation shows up, you can also drag it to view it at a different angle.
  • Works best on Chrome and Firefox.
